About the Family

Early Business Days

Business runs in the family. It was decades ago, when Mr. Hoff pursued his dream, and established Exquisite Family Restaurant.

During the early days, the business faced struggle due to different choices of the customers, and fund shortage.

Nonetheless, with the strong business acumen, Mr. Hoff was able to get on top of the business, and run it for decades! Now, the same business is run by the sons.

The Three Brothers

The restaurant business is currently being run by the three brothers, but headed by the eldest of them.

The business generates more than a million USD per annum, making it one of the lucrative businesses in the vicinity.

Mr. Ted has big plans regarding the business, and wants to improve the overall situation of the business, so that profitability grows from all ends.

Ted works closely with his brothers to ensure the success of the venture.

Way Forward

Ted has high hopes for the business, and really hopes that the company will flourish in the coming years. He is also a huge believer of diversification, and hence likes to include new items to his overall offerings from time to time.

The business expects to expand in order to meet the growing customer demands. With competition rising, Ted thinks he needs to decide about his next step carefully.

Ted is a locally popular figure, and he strives to maintain the same status by enhancing the path his father has shown

Ted collaborates with his brothers, and they manage the day to day operations.
