About

Exquisite Family Restaurant has been operating for more than three decades. It is a family run business currently run by Ted Hoff, who is the eldest of three brothers.

The business was started by Mr. Ted's father as a passion to provide cuisines that are popular among families. Now, even after three decades of successful business, Mr. Ted and his brothers are running the business quite elegantly.

Exquisite Family Restaurant specializes in authentic family food. These foods are not only common in our day to day life, but also in our social gatherings.

Therefore, whether you want to enjoy a dinner with your family, or want to throw a big party for your celebration, Exquisite Family Restaurant is right there for you!
