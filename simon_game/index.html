How to Play

  • - Click on the header (mobile), or press any key (desktop/laptop) to start the game.
  • - One of the red, blue, green, or yellow button will blink, and make sound as the game starts.
  • - You need to click on the colored button that blinked.
  • - As you progress, you need to remember the pattern from first to last, and enter the pattern accordingly.
  • - The game will be over once you misjudge the pattern.
  • - The game tests your memory, and pattern recognition.
  • - Aim to level up as much as you can.
