How to Use Fontology

Stylize your text with fonts!

  • -> Click on Generate Text to view excerpts from shakespearean plays.
  • -> Choose your preferred font from the dropdown menu.
  • -> See the output in your chosen font.
  • -> Once done, you can clear text to remove text from screen.
  • -> You can generate text, and stylize with font as much as you want.
  • -> This is helpful when you are trying to find the right font for your textual needs.
